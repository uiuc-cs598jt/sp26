Course Description

This course introduces students to large language models (LLMs) and their transformative role in modern software engineering. Students will learn how transformer-based models work, how to design effective prompts, how to fine-tune LLMs, and how to integrate them into contemporary workflows. With a focus on real-world applications, students will gain theoretical foundations and hands-on experience with cutting-edge AI tools.

Elements of the Course

Lectures: Foundational concepts & topics

Readings: Curated papers, blog posts, documentation

Hands-on Labs: Guided in-class exercises

Discussions: In-class and/or online

Quizzes & Assignments: Assess progress
